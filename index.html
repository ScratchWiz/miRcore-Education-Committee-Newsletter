                <h2 class="section-title">About Our Newsletter</h2>

                <p>Science Insights is published monthly by the Education Committee of <a href="https://www.mircore.org/" class="mircore-link" target="_blank">miRcore</a>, a 501(c)(3) non-profit research organization dedicated to democratizing medical research and advancing personalized medicine.</p>

                

                <p>Our newsletter is written by and for students participating in miRcore's programs, including the miRcore Volunteer Program (MVP), GIDAS chapters, and various research initiatives. We aim to make complex scientific concepts accessible while showcasing the innovative work being done by high school researchers.</p>

                

                <p><strong>miRcore's mission</strong> is to advance science and medicine by advocating personalized medicine and democratizing medical research. Through our educational programs, we teach computational biology to high school students, enabling them to conduct meaningful research in genomics and contribute to scientific discovery.</p>

                

                <p>Each issue features student research findings, explanations of cutting-edge scientific concepts, interviews with researchers, and updates from miRcore's various programs including summer camps, the Citizen Scientist Sequencing Initiative, and Lab-on-Wheels.</p>
